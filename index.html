    Best scottish ...

  • Whisky

    • For the finest Whisky in Glasgow, look no further than Clydeside Distillary. There has been brewing in Clydeside since the industrial revolution, and also has a fun interactive tour

  • Haggis

    • For the tastiest haggis, it has to be City merchant a flare on an old favorite, this will convert even the greatest skeptics to the dish

  • Fried marsbar

    • A true glaswegian classic, to become a local you must try a fried marsbar. As rich and artery clogging as it sounds, for this experience it has to be Maggie Mays . Open seven days a week and alwasy lively, this is a place which captures Glasgow life.
